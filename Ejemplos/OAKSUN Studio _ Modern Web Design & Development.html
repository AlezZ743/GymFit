Freqently Asked Questions

What makes OAKSUN different from other web studios?

OAKSUN stands out through a commitment to hand-coded, custom solutions built with the super-performant Astro 🚀 framework. No WordPress is used, allowing full control and the ability to optimize for speed, security and performance. The focus on JAMSTACK architecture and Headless CMS ensures your site is always fast, secure, and scalable.

How long does it typically take to complete a website project?

Project timelines vary depending on the complexity and scope of work. A basic website might take 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality or e-commerce integration could take 8-12 weeks or more. A detailed timeline will be provided during the initial consultation.

Do you offer website maintenance services after the site is launched?

Yes, ongoing maintenance and support packages are available to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These options can be discussed as the project nears completion.

How much does a typical project cost?

Pricing varies based on the project's scope and complexity. Each solution is customized and tailored to specific needs and budget. A detailed quote will be provided after the initial consultation.

Can you help with content creation for my website?

While the primary focus is on design and development, guidance in content strategy is available.

What is JAMSTACK, and why do you use it?

JAMSTACK is a modern web development architecture that delivers better performance, higher security, lower cost of scaling, and a better developer experience. It results in websites that are faster, more secure, and easier to scale than traditional architectures.

What is a Headless CMS, and why should I care?

A Headless CMS separates the content management from the front-end presentation. This allows for greater flexibility, improved performance, and the ability to publish content to multiple platforms easily. It's particularly beneficial for businesses that need to manage content across various channels.

How do you ensure the websites you build are secure?

Security is a top priority. Best practices in secure coding, HTTPS encryption, robust authentication methods, and regular system updates are implemented. The JAMSTACK approach inherently reduces many common security vulnerabilities.

Can you help improve my website's search engine rankings?

Yes, SEO best practices are built into every site created. This includes optimizing site structure, improving page load speeds, ensuring mobile responsiveness, and implementing proper meta tags and schemas. Guidance on content optimization for SEO is also provided.

Do you offer e-commerce solutions other than Shopify?

While specializing in custom Shopify solutions, other e-commerce platforms are available if your project requires it. The best options for your specific needs will be discussed during consultation.

How do you handle revisions and feedback during the design process?

The design process is collaborative and iterative. Designs are presented at key milestones with welcomed feedback. The process typically includes 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure complete satisfaction with the final product.

Can you integrate my website with other business tools and software?

Absolutely. Custom integrations with a wide range of business tools are available, from CRM systems to marketing automation platforms. Specific integration needs will be discussed during the planning phase.

What kind of support do you offer after the website is launched?

Various levels of post-launch support are available, from basic maintenance to comprehensive growth packages. This can include regular updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and ongoing optimization.

How do you measure the success of a website project?

Clear, measurable goals are established at the outset of each project. These might include improvements in page load speed, increased conversion rates, higher search engine rankings, or other KPIs specific to your business objectives. Detailed reporting on these metrics is provided post-launch.
